Brand definition

BananaStack

BananaStack is a modern IT studio built for businesses that want practical digital solutions with a clear, memorable identity. The name blends a bright, approachable energy with the layered technology behind apps, AI media, automation, networks, and infrastructure.

Amber gold

This warm accent brings energy, movement, and focus to key moments across the brand.

Deep indigo

This deep digital color reflects intelligence, reliability, and the technology behind every solution.

Charcoal

This strong dark tone gives the brand a grounded, professional feel and helps the wordmark stand out.

Muted gray

This softer gray supports text, secondary details, and interface elements without overpowering the main message.

Logo mark

The logo mark uses three layered curved forms to suggest movement, structure, and technology. It feels modern and distinctive while signaling the breadth of services behind BananaStack.
